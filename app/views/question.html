NHS.UK Redesign How can we warn users about something important?

What are we trying to find out

Our pages have lots of information which meet various needs. How do we highlight important information on a page of health information (including medicines)?

Our method

Popup, Leeds library - 1 June 2018

What we tested
Warning callout version 1 on Medicines page (mobile)
Change name
Number of users
33
Change date of birth
Questions we asked
  1. Do users notice the warning call out in relation to other content on the page?
  2. Are they seeing it with its intended prominence related to other content on page?
  3. Do they understand what it means?
Change contact information
High level findings:
Majority of users didn't see the call out, and when asked they suggested it was an advert.
